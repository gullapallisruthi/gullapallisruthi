Academic Statement

I am a 10th-grade student with a strong interest in mathematics, science, and technology. Through the Active Learning Institute, I am developing important academic skills such as clearly explaining my thinking, reflecting on mistakes, and learning beyond simply relying on answer keys.

I have a deep passion for pursuing a career in the medical field, especially inspired by the medical challenges my family in India has faced. Problems I Noticed & Action Taken

Identifying challenges in my community and taking proactive steps to solve them.

The Problem

Lack of Medical Awareness Among Peers

Many students are interested in medicine but lack understanding of the diverse career paths and public health issues affecting our community.

What I Did

I am organizing a medical-focused club at school to host guest speakers, discuss public health topics, and connect students with shadowing opportunities.

The Problem

Ineffective Note-Taking Strategies

I noticed that many peers, including myself, struggled to retain information because we were just copying slides rather than synthesizing information.

What I Did

I developed a disciplined note-taking system during my coaching sessions and now host events to teach these effective study skills to younger students.

The Problem

Lack of Basic First Aid Knowledge in Kids

I noticed that young children often lack an understanding of basic medicine and CPR, which are critical skills during life-threatening situations.

What I Did

I organized an interactive event where children practiced with CPR dummies and learned the fundamentals of medicine in a hands-on environment.

The Problem

Support for Vulnerable Children

My peers and I noticed that local children's hospitals and shelters for homeless youth were in need of additional resources, funding, and community support.

What We Did

Co-founded "Magic Makers," a volunteer group that organizes fundraisers like hot chocolate stands. We donate proceeds to children's hospitals while earning service hours.
